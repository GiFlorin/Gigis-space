  • 2.4.25 Lessons in Chemistry - Bonnie Garmus
    Book ✧ Music, Drama ★★★★★
    (...)

    I'm speechless. Just perfect, I didn't expect this from any book in my life, almost a study of human life. It manages to be about the same thing that so many books are about, but totally unique.

    I think it's almost impossible not to be moved by the story of Elizabeth trying to make her place in the society of the time. There were times when I didn't agree with her, but at no point did she do anything that I didn't completely understand.

    I swear I'd be a huge fan of Dinner Time, I really want a physical copy of this book just so I can nurture the affection I have for this book, it's one of the few I'd read again just to be able to pay attention to every detail.

    I love the way the book is constructed: the beginning, middle and end are almost their own stories, and at the same time, each part only seems to get better, impressive.

    I'll be waiting like a puppy for Bonnie's next book!
